A simple and rewarding checkout experience your customers love. No account, routing, or microdeposits needed.

## Step 1: Choose Magic at checkout

- Customer completes shopping and selects the Magic Pay by Bank option at the checkout.
- Magic will open it's hosted page and will prompt with Two factor authentication page, where customer can authenticate with their phone number.

## Step 2: Authenticate with Plaid

- We use Plaid to link customer’s bank account, just like Venmo or Cashapp. It’s free, seamless, and secure.
- Once customer successfully authenticate, then they will need to authenticate with bank and need to link their payment method with Plaid.

## Step 3: Pay with Magic

- Supercharge existing subscription discounts with Magic's additional rewards at checkout!
- On successfully linking bank with plaid, customer will redirect to complete purchase page where customer will process with __Pay Now__ button and it will redirect to relatively success or error page based on payment response.
